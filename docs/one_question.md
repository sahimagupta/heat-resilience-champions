# The One Question

> **If heat already kills 546,000 people a year, costs the global economy $1.09 trillion, exposes 2.41 billion workers, disrupts the education of 242 million children — and we already have interventions proven to reduce heat mortality by 80% — then what exactly are we waiting for?**

---

This single question contains:

- **The mortality crisis** → 546,000 deaths/year (Lancet Countdown 2025)
- **The economic case** → $1.09 trillion lost (ILO 2024)
- **The workforce scale** → 2.41 billion workers at risk (ILO 2024)
- **The children's future** → 242 million educations disrupted (UNICEF 2024)
- **The proof of solution** → 80% reduction achievable (Lancet Europe 2024)
- **The accountability gap** → *What are we waiting for?*

It works for any audience — funders, governments, media, communities — because it frames the issue as not a knowledge gap but an action gap.

The data exists. The solutions exist. The question is whether we deploy them.
